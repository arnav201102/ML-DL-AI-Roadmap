### 1. Define dot product.

a·b = |a||b|cosθ

### 2. What does the dot product measure?

Angle and similarity between vectors.

### 3. When is dot product zero?

When vectors are perpendicular.

### 5. What is a·a equal to?

|a|²

### 6. Define cross product.

A vector perpendicular to two given vectors.

### 7. In which space is cross product defined?

Only ℝ³.

### 8. What does |a×b| represent?

Area of the parallelogram.

### 9. Compute (1,0,0)×(0,1,0).

(0,0,1)

### 10. Why is cross product zero for parallel vectors?

Because sinθ = 0.

### 11. What is the right-hand rule?

A method to determine cross product direction.

### 12. Dot product application in ML?

Cosine similarity.

### 13. Cross product application in physics?

Torque and angular momentum.

### 14. Can dot product be negative?

Yes, for obtuse angles.

### 15. What does the sign of dot product indicate?

Direction relationship between vectors.
